GREEK-STYLE PIZZA

Characterized by its thick, wettish dough, greasy cheese, tomato sauce with a strong taste of oregano and a topping of delicious greek ingredients. Baked in a heavily greased pan, which results in a lacy, crisp edge and fried bottom crust.

MARGHERITA PIZZA

Known for its ingredients representing the colours of the Italian flag: red tomato sauce, white mozzarella and fresh green basil. When all of these delicious flavours are combined on a hand-kneaded pizza base, a universally-adored pizza is created.

Italy, Europe

PIZZA ALLA DIAVOLA

We pride ourselves for making the most delicious pizza in Kokata. Diavola is a variety of Italian pizza that is traditionally topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, spicy salami, and hot chili peppers. Black olives are optional and can be added for extra flavor.
